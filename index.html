Brief Introduction

Welcome to Coral County Roleplay

Coral County Roleplay is a rapidly growing roleplay community available on roblox for PC players. Coral County Roleplay is a game set in California, United States. The reason our development team picked out this location is because of the tropical and desert-like environment which can make the game have a unique atmosphere.
Coral County Roleplay has tons of amazingly-designed infrastructure to show off, aswell as the beautiful landscape surrounding it.

Our rank scheme

We use a common rank scheme so it is easy to understand. However, we know that some people don't know what our actual rank scheme is. Thats why we have created this list.

- Member = A standard member who has joined the Discord

- Trusted Member = A member who is trusted by our Community Management

- Supporter = A member who has boosted our Discord Server

- Trainee Staff = A handpicked staff member who has recently joinrd and are undergoing the training

- Moderator = A staff member who has finished basic training

- Senior Moderator = A moderator who has been in our staff tram for at least 5 weeks

- Administrator = A senior moderator who has shown constant respect towards higher ups and members

- Senior Administrator = A senior administrator is a handpicked administrator who is constantly active on be game. This is an extremely hard rank to achieve

- Community Manager = A staff member who manages the Game and Discord Server, they are responsible for everything that happens. This is an impossible rank to achieve

New Player Guide

We know that new players may find it difficult to play our game. That's why we have created a system for players to get to know the game's mechanics.

Step One: Getting Money

In order to get a car, you need to have a job. You can start earning money using these civilian jobs.

- Delivery Driver = $1 every 1 second

Step two: Purchasing and Spawning Vehicles

All players need to purchase a vehicle before they can spawn it. You need money for this (view step one for information on how to make money)


Where are the vehicle spawners?

You can find the vehicle spawners relatively close to your team spawn. They are usually the same color as your team.

Revamped Emergency Vehicle Controls

As we continue to revamp vehicles, their controls change.

J = Activate Lights
R = Activate Wail Siren
T = Activate Yelp Siren
G = Activate Priority Siren
Y = Activate Automatic Siren
H (while no siren is activated) = Activate Manual Siren (hold)
H (while a siren is activated) = Horn
F = Horn
- = Activate Primary Dual Siren
= = Activate Secondary Dual Siren
[ = Activate Alley Lights
] = Activate TakeDown Lights
